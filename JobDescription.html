Cloud Engineer

We are seeking a Junior Cloud Engineer to assist in designing, implementing, and maintaining cloud-based infrastructure and applications. The role involves collaborating with senior engineers to ensure the smooth operation of cloud environments, troubleshooting technical issues, and deploying innovative solutions. This is an exciting opportunity for someone eager to start a career in cloud engineering, gaining hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies, while growing their career in a supportive and diverse environment.

What we are looking for:

  • Hands-on experience with technical problem-solving to debug issues across AWS and Azure through study, labs, personal projects or interships.
  • Knowledge of AWS/Azure services and security topics (e.g. IAM).
  • Familiarity with scripting languagues (e.g. Bash or Python) or Infrastructure-as-Code tools (e.g., Terraform, CloudFormation).
  • A strong passion for cloud-based solutions, with a commitment to continuous learning and professional growth.
  • Good interpersonal skills and a positive, team-oriented attitude, contributing to effective collaboration with colleagues and stakeholders.

Cloud Systems Administrator

Our team is seeking an experienced level 2 administrator versed in Microsoft Azure and AWS cloud services to join our expanding cloud network division. An ideal candidate would have strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills, be able to navigate and implement a wide variety of cloud-based infrastructure solutions. This role will require daily administration, maintenance, and onsite support for our cloud-based systems, ensuring their availability, security, and performance.

What we are looking for:

  • Confidence with Microsoft Azure and AWS cloud platforms.
  • Established system administration skills for Windows Server environments in the cloud.
  • Experience in cloud resource management, including provisioning, scaling, and cost optimization for virtual machines, storage, and databases in both AWS and Azure.
  • Proficient in cloud automation using tools like Terraform and CloudFormation to automate cloud infrastructure deployments and updates.
  • Solid knowledge of cloud security best practices, including configuring IAM roles, firewalls, VPNs, and managing cloud-based security controls to ensure compliance and data protection.

Why sign up?

  • Flexible working arrangements, with the option to work from home three days a week.
  • A home office setup allowance.
  • An additional two weeks of annual leave.
  • Health & wellbeing benefits including discounts on gym memberships.
